Wondrwall Smart Wallpaper

Wondrwall is the world's first multifunctional smart wall socket. Wondrwall integrates technology into walls which functions as light switches, Wi-Fi boosters, thermostats and motion sensors. We have also created the first integrated OLED lighting into wallpaper which makes the walls come even more alive with light.
